Mashed potatoes

Description

Mashed potatoes is a simple, but delicious dish that includes six to seven potatoes, salt, pepper, milk and butter all mixed together.

The potatoes are boiled to a point where they are easily smashed by a for.

Adding salt and pepper then milk so that the potatoes will be fluffy.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  1. Get a medium sized pot
  2. Skin your potatoes
  3. Cut the potatoes into small to medium sized chunks
  4. Fill the pot with water and wait till it starts a rolling boil
  5. Add your potatoes into the pot
  6. Wait till the potatoes come to a boil testing your potatoes to see if they are smashable
  7. Add a 1 to 2 table spoons of butter
  8. Add salt and pepper to your liking
  9. Then add a splash of milk
  10. Use a electronic whisker to mix the mashed potatoes
  11. Add more salt and pepper to taste and you are done
